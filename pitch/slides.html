The problem

I made none of my own videos —
because 80% of it isn't creating

  • Every video I ship: I didn't edit it, didn't make the thumbnail, didn't write the captions. Agents did. I'm non-technical.
  • One video really means: topic → script → shoot → edit → thumbnail → three platform captions → three uploads
  • Only the topic and the shoot are creating. The rest is hours of grunt work per video — post daily and you've opened an unpaid sweatshop with yourself inside
