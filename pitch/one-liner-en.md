# Creator OS · One-Liners + Elevator Pitch (English)

> Event: BUIDL OPC Hackathon SG
> Subtitle: Your One-Person Media Company

---

## One-liner × 3 versions

**Version A (for investors — the value)**
> Creator OS is the operating system for a creator's one-person company: one idea in, a three-platform launch kit out. The human only judges and presses publish.

**Version B (for technical mentors — the mechanism)**
> Seven Agents chained into a content pipeline, plus a calibration loop — the AI blind-predicts before you publish, reconciles against real numbers after, and gets sharper on your account every time.

**Version C (for anyone — the story)**
> My videos — the editing, the thumbnails, the captions — I made none of it. Agents did. One person, non-technical, running a whole media company.

---

## 30-second elevator pitch

> For people who make content, 80% of the time isn't creating — it's scripting, editing, cutting thumbnails, writing a caption for every platform, and uploading to each. Repetitive grunt work.
>
> Creator OS hands that pipeline to seven Agents. Idea comes in; out come the script, the cut, the thumbnail, and captions for three platforms, and the upload page fills itself. The human does two things — review and clear, then press publish.
>
> And it has something no one else does: before you publish, the AI blind-predicts whether the video will land; after you publish, it pulls the real numbers and reconciles. It gets sharper on your account every time. That calibration data is yours — switch tools and you can't take it with you.
>
> I'm the first user. One video went from hours to ten minutes. One person plus a crew of Agents is a whole MCN — that's the One Person Company this hackathon is about.
