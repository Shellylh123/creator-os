============================================
PART 1 — 给 MiniMax 的旁白正文（4 段分别生成 4 个音频文件）
建议音色：沉稳男声或清晰女声，语速 1.0-1.05，美音
============================================

【Segment 1 · Copy Pack · ~20s】

This is Creator OS — your one-person media company. The whole interface is bilingual: English or Chinese, one toggle.
Every tool works standalone. I paste in a video transcript, hit Generate — and there it is. Rednote title, body and tags. Douyin title. An X post. Thumbnail titles. All at once. This used to take me an hour. Now? Ten seconds.

【Segment 2 · 流水线 + 口播剪辑（主打）· ~35s】

And that was just one station. Here's the full production line: Idea, Script, Shoot, Edit, Cover, Copy, Publish. It handles two kinds of videos: pure talking-head, and knowledge videos with B-roll.
Talking-head is the production lane — I run my real account on it. One click starts preprocessing: normalize, transcribe, AI sentence-split. Fifty-six seconds, unattended. Then I review the transcript like a document — delete a bad take, fix a word — and the final cut renders in one pass, captions burned in. My hands-on time per video: about ten minutes. It used to be hours.

【Segment 3 · B-roll 简演 · ~15s】

For knowledge videos, there's an early B-roll lane too. The Agent reads the transcript, marks where visuals belong, and pulls stock candidates. I pick — it composites. The Agent does the grunt work. I make the calls.

【Segment 4 · 发布台 · ~25s】

Last stop — the Publish Desk. One click, and an automation browser uploads the video and fills in the title, the body, the tags. Everything. But watch this button. The Publish button is always pressed by a human. The AI gets everything ready — but the one press that sends it into the world, that's a person's call.
That's Creator OS. Humans judge. Agents execute. One person, a crew of Agents, a whole media company.

============================================
PART 2 — 录屏动作对时表（4 段分开录，每段结尾多停 2-3 秒）
QuickTime 静音录制，浏览器全屏，左下角语言切到 EN
============================================

【视频 1 · 约 22s】
- 停在 Copy Pack 工具页 → 点左下角语言切换（EN↔中文来回一次）
- 粘贴转录稿（提前复制好）→ 点 Generate multi-platform copy → 结果出来后鼠标扫过四个输出卡片

【视频 2 · 约 38s】（主打段，从容一点）
- 点侧边栏《如果自动化创作视频》（纯口播项目）→ 鼠标从左到右扫过 7 个工位
- 点「New video」弹层，展示两张类型卡片（Talking-head / Knowledge·B-roll）→ 关掉弹层
- 进工位④ → 展示「Start ClipLab preprocessing」按钮和下方日志
- 新标签页打开 http://localhost:8900 （审核页）→ 勾掉一句、双击改一个字（演示即可）
- 切回来展示成片（该项目发布台里的成片路径，或直接播放成品文件几秒）

【视频 3 · 约 17s】
- 点《Fable5 最后一天》项目 → 工位④
- 展示 B-roll 标注点列表和素材缩略图 → 点选一张候选图
- 往下滚到 Composite result，播放合成视频 2-3 秒

【视频 4 · 约 28s】
- 该项目工位⑦ 发布台 → 点视频号 Auto-fill upload
- 切到自动化浏览器窗口看它自动填（若等待久，可先录后面、单独补拍"已填好"画面）
- 鼠标移到「发表」按钮上悬停 3 秒，不点
- 切回 Creator OS 首页定格

录完把 4 个视频 + 4 个音频丢进一个文件夹（如 ~/Downloads/demo素材/），按 v1-v4、a1-a4 命名最好，发我路径。
