============================================
PART 1 — 给 MiniMax 的旁白正文（4 段分别生成 4 个音频文件）
建议音色：沉稳男声或清晰女声，语速 1.0-1.05，美音
总长约 85 秒
============================================

【Segment 1 · Copy Pack · ~15s】

This is Creator OS — your one-person media company. Every tool works standalone. I paste in a video transcript, hit Generate — and there: titles, captions and tags for three platforms, plus thumbnail titles. Ten seconds. It's bilingual, by the way.

【Segment 2 · 流水线 + 口播剪辑（主打）· ~32s】

That was one station of seven: Idea, Script, Shoot, Edit, Cover, Copy, Publish. Two lanes — talking-head, or knowledge videos with B-roll. Talking-head is the production lane; my real account runs on it. One click: transcribe and split — fifty-six seconds, unattended. Then I review the transcript like a document: delete a bad take, fix a word — and the final cut renders with captions burned in. Ten minutes of my time. It used to be hours.

【Segment 3 · B-roll 简演 · ~13s】

For knowledge videos, an early B-roll lane: the Agent marks where visuals belong and pulls stock candidates. I pick — it composites. The Agent does the grunt work. I make the calls.

【Segment 4 · 发布台 · ~22s】

Last stop: the Publish Desk. One click — a browser uploads the video and fills in everything: title, captions, tags. But this Publish button is always pressed by a human. The AI gets everything ready; the press that sends it into the world is a person's call. Creator OS. Humans judge. Agents execute.

============================================
PART 2 — 录屏动作对时表（4 段分开录，每段结尾多停 2-3 秒）
QuickTime 静音录制，浏览器全屏，左下角语言切到 EN
============================================

【视频 1 · 约 17s】
- 停在 Copy Pack 工具页 → 粘贴转录稿（提前复制好）→ 点 Generate multi-platform copy
- 等结果出来 → 鼠标扫过四个输出卡片 → 顺手点一下左下角语言切换（EN↔中文一个来回）

【视频 2 · 约 34s】（主打段，从容一点）
- 点侧边栏《如果自动化创作视频》（纯口播项目）→ 鼠标从左到右扫过 7 个工位
- 进工位④ → 展示「Start ClipLab preprocessing」按钮和日志
- 新标签页开 http://localhost:8900 审核页 → 勾掉一句、双击改一个字
- 切回工位④ → 滚到「Final cut preview」播放器 → 播放带字幕成片 2-3 秒
  （录制前先做一次：工位④点"剪辑已完成"按钮登记成品，播放器才会出现）

【视频 3 · 约 15s】
- 点《Fable5 最后一天》项目 → 工位④ → 展示 B-roll 标注点和素材缩略图 → 点选一张
- 滚到 Composite result 播放合成视频 2 秒

【视频 4 · 约 24s】
- 该项目工位⑦ 发布台 → 点视频号 Auto-fill upload → 切到自动化浏览器看它自动填
  （等待久可后补拍"已填好"画面）
- 鼠标移到「发表」按钮悬停 3 秒，不点 → 切回 Creator OS 首页定格

录完把 4 个视频 + 4 个音频丢进一个文件夹（如 ~/Downloads/demo素材/），
命名 v1-v4、a1-a4 最好，发我路径，我 ffmpeg 合成出成品。
